Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

An everyday task we are going to write Psuedocode is: Cooking Chicken and rice

  1. Grab Chicken
  2. Season Chicken
  3. Put chicken in pan
  4. Flip Chicken
  5. While it is flipped, put rice in pot and boil
  6. When the chicken is done, put aside
  7. When rice is done boiling, put aside
  8. Put both chicken and rice on a plate
  9. Eat


Task 2: Pseudocode a simple computer game

Choice of computer game: Chess

Psuedocode here:

  1. Generate Board
  2. Generate Chess pieces for player 1
  3. Generate Chess pieces for player 2
  4. Determine if the chess pieces are allowed on that space
  5. Eliminate chess pieces when crossed by either player
  6. Create a counter of how many chess pieces lost
  7. Then determine winner
